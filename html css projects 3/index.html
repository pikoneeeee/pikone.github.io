About us

About us

Elegant is a Kazakhstani brand of men's clothing and accessories, which includes many boutiques in Kazakhstan and Russia. Since the foundation of the brand in 1917, its ambassadors have been: Akhmet Baytursynov, Dinmuhammad Akhmeduly Konaev, Olzhas Suleimenov and others.

Our product is designer suits, shoes made of genuine calfskin and stylish accessories for flawless completed images.
